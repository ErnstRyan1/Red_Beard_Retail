Pie Menu Keybindings

  • Unit Switcher

    attr.formPopover {rb_unit_switcher:sheet}
  • hit Alt-7

    • Switch your units for the scene.

    Typical Usecase

    This pie menu will switch your units, and you can activate the dimenisions tool.

    UV Unwrapping Pie Menu

    attr.formPopover {rb_uv_unwrapping:sheet}
  • hit Shift-0

    • This menu will let you quickly preform common unwrapping tasks even when you don't have an UV window open.

    Typical Usecase

    While your building your packaging you can use the Unwrap and Relex command on any polygons or line segements you have selected to quickly build your UV unwraps. You can use the rectangle, fit, and align tools to clean up your unwrap. You can also export your UVs directly from this piemenu.
